Zanka no Tachi (残火の太刀)

Wielder: Genryūsai Shigekuni Yamamoto
Description: This Bankai focuses all the flames of his Zanpakutō into the blade, raising its temperature to an incomprehensible level, capable of incinerating anything.
Kannonbiraki Benihime Aratame (観音開紅姫改メ)

Wielder: Kisuke Urahara
Description: It allows Urahara to restructure anything it touches, from his body to inanimate objects, essentially reshaping matter to his will.
Senbonzakura Kageyoshi (千本桜景厳)

Wielder: Byakuya Kuchiki
Description: Transforms Byakuya's sword into thousands of tiny, razor-sharp cherry blossom petals, allowing for deadly and precise attacks.
Daiguren Hyōrinmaru (大紅蓮氷輪丸)

Wielder: Tōshirō Hitsugaya
Description: Summons massive ice dragons and grants Hitsugaya full control over water and ice, allowing for incredible freezing abilities.
Katen Kyōkotsu: Karamatsu Shinjū (花天狂骨枯松心中)

Wielder: Shunsui Kyōraku
Description: Traps the enemy in a reality where they must play deadly games based on children’s stories, with fatal consequences for losing.
Tensa Zangetsu (天鎖斬月)

Wielder: Ichigo Kurosaki
Description: Compresses Ichigo's spiritual pressure into a black sword, enhancing his speed and power dramatically for close-combat battles.
Shūkō no Kotowari (終括の理)

Wielder: Jūshirō Ukitake
Description: His Bankai ability isn’t fully revealed in the anime or manga, but it’s tied to controlling and manipulating energy flows for defensive measures.
Konjiki Ashisogi Jizō (金色疋殺地蔵)

Wielder: Mayuri Kurotsuchi
Description: Summons a giant, terrifying caterpillar-like creature with a baby's face, capable of releasing deadly poison in its surroundings.
Minazuki (皆尽)

Wielder: Retsu Unohana
Description: Although not fully revealed, it is known to transform into a giant manta ray-like creature and has incredible healing powers.
Hakka no Togame (白霞罸)

Wielder: Rukia Kuchiki
Description: Lowers Rukia’s body temperature to absolute zero, freezing everything around her. It's one of the most elegant and lethal Bankai with extreme precision.
